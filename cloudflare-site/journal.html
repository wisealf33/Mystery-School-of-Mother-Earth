Quick answers

Simple questions people might ask before they join.

Do I need to be local?

No. The project is designed to grow online first, with local circles and events added over time.

Can I browse without joining?

Yes. The public side exists so people can find useful events, resources, and aligned opportunities before joining.

How do I keep up?

Use the newsletter, the journal, and the directory. Together they create a simple path from discovery to deeper involvement.
