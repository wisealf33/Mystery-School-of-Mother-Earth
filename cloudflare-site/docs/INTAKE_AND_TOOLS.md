# Intake And Tools

## First Data Buckets

Use separate tags or form fields for:

- Founding member
- Vendor / practitioner
- Circle builder
- Market vendor
- Teacher / workshop host
- Newsletter contributor
- Volunteer
- Sponsor / partner
- Chapter 001 / southern South Chicagoland
- Peotone / Kankakee / Wilmington area
- Online only

## Minimum Tool Stack

**Website:** current static site, later Carrd, Webflow, WordPress, Ghost, or custom app.

**Email:** MailerLite or ConvertKit.

**Intake tracking:** Airtable, Notion, or Google Sheets.

**Community:** Facebook Page plus Facebook Group first. Later consider Circle, Mighty Networks, Discord, or a private member portal.

**Payments:** Do not activate until membership terms are ready. Later consider Stripe, PayPal, Square, or a membership platform.

**Forms:** MailerLite/ConvertKit forms for simple newsletter signup; Airtable or Tally for richer vendor/practitioner intake.

## Recommended First Setup

1. Create MailerLite or ConvertKit account.
2. Create one general founding interest form.
3. Create one vendor/practitioner interest form.
4. Create tags for each interest type.
5. Add form links to website and Facebook.
6. Track richer details in Airtable or Notion.
7. Send the weekly newsletter consistently.

## Website To Newsletter To Facebook Flow

Public post sends people to the website. Website explains the umbrella and invites founding interest. Form adds them to the newsletter and tags their interest. Newsletter sends them back to member opportunities, directory submissions, circle updates, and future market planning. Facebook group creates conversation and warmth between newsletter issues.

## Private Association Framing

Use public wording for mission and invitation. Keep specific member offers, promotion schedules, event participation rules, and vendor opportunities framed as association/member pathways.
