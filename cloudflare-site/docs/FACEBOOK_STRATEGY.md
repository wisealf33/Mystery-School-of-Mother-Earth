# Facebook Strategy

## What To Create First

Create the public Facebook Page first:

**Mystery School of Mother Earth Foundation**

Use it as the official public signal for the project. After the page has its first posts and basic identity, create a private or visible Facebook Group:

**Mother Earth Community Circle**

The page is the public broadcast channel. The group is the relationship space.

## Public Page Description

Mystery School of Mother Earth Foundation is an emerging private membership community for earth stewardship, natural living, local resilience, gardening, green business, practical earth skills, healing arts, spiritual growth, mystery-school learning, and real community.

We are beginning online first, with a weekly newsletter, member directory, founding member intake, vendor/practitioner pathways, and future Mother Earth Circles that can take root locally. Our first local chapter is expected to grow in the southern South Chicagoland area around Peotone, Kankakee, Wilmington, and nearby communities, while the larger vision is built to support many local circles over time.

## Group Description

Mother Earth Community Circle is a welcoming discussion space connected to Mystery School of Mother Earth Foundation. This group is for people interested in natural living, earth stewardship, local resilience, gardening, green business, practical skills, healing arts, spiritual growth, and building real community.

The group is a public-facing bridge into the private membership association. Member opportunities, vendor/practitioner promotion, directory placement, market participation, and internal project details are handled through the association.

## First 10 Post Ideas

1. Public introduction: what Mystery School of Mother Earth Foundation is becoming.
2. Ask: What earth skill do you most want to learn this year?
3. Founding member invitation focused on requesting membership, with no public price language.
4. Vendor/practitioner callout for growers, makers, healers, teachers, and green businesses.
5. Explain the weekly newsletter and invite member spotlights.
6. Chapter 001 local circle seed post for Peotone, Kankakee, Wilmington, and the southern South Chicagoland area.
7. Mother Earth Market concept post.
8. Spirit Bucks explanation in plain language.
9. Community values post: grounded, respectful, practical, spiritually alive.
10. Ask for referrals: who should be part of this from the beginning?

## Public Introduction Post

I am beginning to gather founding interest for Mystery School of Mother Earth Foundation, an emerging private membership community centered around earth stewardship, natural living, local resilience, gardening, green business, practical earth skills, healing arts, spiritual growth, mystery-school learning, and real community.

The first step is online: a member community, weekly newsletter, directory, and pathways for vendors, practitioners, teachers, growers, makers, supporters, and circle-builders. Over time, the vision is to help local Mother Earth Circles take root, beginning with Chapter 001 in the southern South Chicagoland area around Peotone, Kankakee, Wilmington, and nearby communities.

If this speaks to you, you can join the founding interest list, share what you offer, or simply follow along as the foundation takes shape.

## Posting Rhythm

- 3 public page posts per week
- 3 to 5 group conversation prompts per week
- 1 member/vendor/practitioner spotlight per week once applicants exist
- 1 newsletter reminder per week
- 1 local circle or market planning update per week
